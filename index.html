ABOUT US

Explore the Journey That Defines Us.

Eatitude began as a Food & Beverage Business Consulting. Serving the F&B Industry for over 2 decades, founded on a passion for transforming traditional dining experiences.

Today, we stand as global leaders in restaurant consulting, helping clients around the world innovate and thrive in the competitive food and beverage industry.

Our mission is to empower food and beverage establishments to maximize their potential. We provide comprehensive solutions that save time, reduce costs, and increase profitability, helping our clients succeed in an ever-evolving marketplace.

About the Founder !

Chef Koushik Shankar, famously known as The Mad Chef, discovered his passion for cooking at the age of five. A graduate of IHM Bangalore, he has over 25 years of experience in the F&B industry

Koushik has collaborated with prominent figures like Mukesh Ambani and Amitabh Bachchan, and has been instrumental in launching numerous successful restaurants across India.

His innovative approach to fusion cuisine and appearances as a judge on shows like MasterChef Tamil, Cook With Comali & Top Cooku Dupe Cooku have solidified his reputation as a true culinary icon.

ABOUT US

Explore the Journey That Defines Us.

Chef Koushik S., famously known as The Mad Chef, found his love for cooking at just five years old. A graduate of IHM Bangalore with over 25 years in the F&B industry, he has built a reputation for creativity and innovation. A defining moment came in 2003, when Mukesh Ambani introduced him as The Mad Chef to Amitabh Bachchan—setting the stage for a bold culinary journey.

From launching successful restaurants across India to creating experimental fusion dishes, Chef Koushik is known for breaking boundaries in food. His work has reached global kitchens, and he's collaborated with top names in the industry. He's also a familiar face on TV, serving as a judge on shows like MasterChef Tamil, Cook With Comali, and Top Cooku Dupe Cooku.

Eatitude, founded by Chef Koushik, began as a food and beverage consulting company with a mission to elevate dining experiences. Today, it's a globally recognized name in restaurant consulting, helping brands streamline operations, cut costs, and grow profitably in a fast-changing industry
